**How long does the battery last?**
A full school day (6-8 hours) of typical classroom use. We recommend charging overnight.

**Are the tablets durable?**
The included protective cover adds significant protection. They're designed for classroom use.

**Can students take tablets home?**
Yes. MDM management continues wherever the device has internet access. Schools can configure policies for home use.
