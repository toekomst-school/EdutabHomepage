## Why Schools Need a Different Applications Marketplace

### The traditional software problem

Traditional software is designed for consumers, not classrooms:

**Privacy Issues**
- Apps request unnecessary permissions
- Tracking and advertising built into many apps
- Data flows to servers and resides outside Europe
- GDPR compliance is questionable at best

**Inappropriate Content**
- Games and entertainment mixed with education
- Age-inappropriate suggestions
- Ads in "educational" apps
- In-app purchases targeting children

**Commercial Company Account Requirement**
- Every device needs a commercial company account
- Account management overhead for IT
- Commercial companies collect data on every download and use

**No School Control**
- Schools can't control which apps students see
- No way to curate a school-specific catalog
- Updates happen automatically, sometimes breaking working ecosystems

## Common Questions

**Can I use the Edutab App Marketplace without EduTab tablets?**
Yes. The Edutab App Marketplace works on any Android 8+ device. It's completely independent of which tablets you use.

**Can I use the Edutab App Marketplace without EduTab Portal (MDM)?**
Yes. You can use the Edutab App Marketplace with just a free account. Portal integration adds features like silent deployment and catalog control.

**Are all apps free?**
Most apps in the curated catalog are free. Some educational apps may be premium, but there are no in-app purchases or hidden costs for ads.

**Can students download any app from the store?**
Schools control their catalog. Students only see apps their school has approved, not the full master catalog.

**How often are new apps added?**
We regularly review and add new educational apps. Schools can also request specific apps for consideration.

**What if an app I need isn't in the Edutab App Marketplace?**
Contact us with requests. We review suggested apps and add them if they meet our standards. You can also upload private apps.

**Can students install apps outside the Edutab App Marketplace?**
Through EduTab Portal, schools can restrict app installation to only the Edutab App Marketplace, preventing unauthorized app downloads.
