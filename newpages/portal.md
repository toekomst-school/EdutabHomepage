## Common Questions

**How long does setup take?**
Portal access is immediate. First device enrollment takes minutes. Full fleet deployment depends on your device count and desired configuration.

**Do teachers need training?**
The teacher interface is designed to be intuitive. Most teachers are comfortable within one class session. Documentation is available for specific features.

**Can we use Portal without EduTab tablets?**
Yes. Portal works with any Android device running Android 8 or later.

**What about Chromebooks?**
EduTab Portal focuses on Android devices. Chromebooks require different management solutions.

**How many admin users can we have?**
Unlimited. All pricing tiers include unlimited administrators and teachers.

**Is there a limit on groups?**
No. Create as many groups as your organization needs.

**What happens if we exceed our device count?**
Add devices anytime. Pricing adjusts automatically, if you cross into a lower tier, all devices benefit from the lower rate.
