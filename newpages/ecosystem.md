## Common Questions

**Do I need to buy EduTab tablets to use the ecosystem?**
No. Edutab Portal and Edutab App Marketplace work with your existing Android devices. Tablets are optional but provide the smoothest experience.

**Can I use just one part of the ecosystem?**
Yes. Edutab App Marketplace is standalone and free. Edutab Portal works without our tablets. Each component has value independently.

**What if I'm already using another MDM?**
You can migrate to EduTab Portal. We provide guidance on transitioning from other MDM solutions.

**How does support work with an integrated ecosystem?**
One point of contact for everything. No finger-pointing between vendors. If something doesn't work, we own the solution.

**Is the ecosystem locked to EduTab?**
No vendor lock-in. Edutab Portal is open source (self-hostable). Apps are standard APKs. Tablets are standard Android. You can leave if you want—we just make sure you don't want to.
